About Me

About Me

After graduating from the University of Pittsburgh in 2020 with a B.S. in bioengineering and a minor in mechanical engineering, I moved to Tucson, AZ, where I am currently a R&D Engineer at the University of Arizona. My research is focused on hand biomechanics, and I have experience in writing programs for image segmentation, recruiting subjects for clinical research, and scientific writing. I am interested in image processing and machine learning, and I am looking to further develop my programming skills. I mainly work with LabView and Python, but also have experience in MATLAB and Java.
