About Me

About Me

I am a research and development engineer in the Hand Research Lab at the University of Arizona, where my research is focused on hand biomechanics. My work involves image processing, robotics, and data analysis, and I am currently leading a project to develop a machine learning model for analyzing carpal tunnel morphology from ultrasound images. I mainly work in Python and LabView, but also have experience in Java and MATLAB. Outside of engineering, I foster dogs and love anything outdoors-related!
